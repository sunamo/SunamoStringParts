# NuGet Alternatives to SunamoStringParts

This document lists popular NuGet packages that provide similar functionality to SunamoStringParts.

## Overview

String parts and splitting

## Alternative Packages

### System.String.Split
- **NuGet**: System.Runtime
- **Purpose**: Built-in splitting
- **Key Features**: Split by char, string, regex

### System.Text.RegularExpressions
- **NuGet**: System.Text.RegularExpressions
- **Purpose**: Pattern-based splitting
- **Key Features**: Regex.Split()

### MoreLINQ
- **NuGet**: morelinq
- **Purpose**: Advanced splitting
- **Key Features**: Split, Batch operations

## Comparison Notes

String.Split() covers most splitting needs. Regex.Split for complex patterns.

## Choosing an Alternative

Consider these alternatives based on your specific needs:
- **System.String.Split**: Built-in splitting
- **System.Text.RegularExpressions**: Pattern-based splitting
- **MoreLINQ**: Advanced splitting
